WORLD TRADE CENTER CHENNAI

ABOUT

The BRIGADE WORLD TRADE CENTER,CHENNAI is a 28-storeyed commercial and residential centre in chennai,India.Located at Perungudi, it was made operational in March 2020. The center consists of 170,000 square meters(1,800000)of office space. The complex also includes a conference/exhibition centre. The towers are IGBC LEED platinumm and USGBC LEED Gold certified. The center is a member of the WORLD TRADE CENTERS ASSOCIATION(WTCA).
